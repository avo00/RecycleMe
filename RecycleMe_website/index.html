Here's the Problem

Environmental issues such as global warming, pollution, and climate change is happening at an unprecedented rate and affecting the overall ecosystems.
According to the UN, approximately 11.2 billion tonnes of solid waste is collected every year, contributing about 5% of greenhouse gas emissions. The waste that are not recycled accumulates as landfills in the natural environment. Reycling can prevent the emission of carbon dioxide and save water, trees, and other valuable resources.

What You Can Do

To make a real difference in the environment, every single person should ACT NOW.
Our mission is to inform people about the actions that makes a real difference in protecting the environment.
Here are some simple & easy ways of saving the environment from now on.

1. Recycle Your Waste

Did you know that the majority of waste we produce is recyclable? It is best not to produce waste as much as possible, but when you do have waste, recycle it properly so that it can be reused again as a different product. Recycling is a good practice because it can reduce the emission of greenhouse gases, which is one of the great contributors to global warming. Be sure to follow the guidelines of recycling in your area.

2. Use Less Plastic

Plastic can be very harmful for our environment and willdlife. It can destroy marine life and consequently affect the overall ecosystems too. There are many ways we can reduce the plastic consumption such as using your own water bottles or coffee cups, packing lunch in reusable containers, saying no to plastic utensils or wrappers, bringing your resuable bags when shopping, and more!

3. Be Environmentally Conscious

It is important that you understand that you can be part of the solution by being responsible of the environment around you.
